FAQ

How does the scan work?

We hash your email, send only the first five characters to the HIBP API, then locally compare suffixes—your full address never leaves your browser.

Is this free?

100 % free for personal use. Power users can upgrade for bulk scans and SMS alerts.

Will you store my email?

Never. All look-ups are done client-side; nothing is written to our servers.
